About Me

Data Analyst passionate about translating data into meaningful insights.

Hello! I’m Ammar Khan, a data analyst with a strong foundation in Python, SQL, and data visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI. I enjoy exploring data, uncovering patterns, and delivering clear, actionable insights.

My journey into data analytics began through hands-on projects ranging from e-commerce price tracking to building dashboards for global health data. I’ve worked on:

  • Scraping and tracking Amazon product prices with automated email alerts.
  • Building Tableau dashboards to visualize COVID-19 trends across countries.
  • Performing exploratory data analysis on Netflix datasets to understand content dynamics.
  • Developing a Hospital Management app in Salesforce, customizing workflows to enhance patient care.

I’m continuously learning advanced analytics and machine learning techniques, aiming to bridge business questions with data-driven answers. Beyond numbers, I value storytelling—transforming raw data into stories that drive decisions.

In my free time, you might find me exploring new datasets, improving my design skills in Illustrator, or diving into a good book on behavioral economics.

Let’s connect and turn data into impact!
